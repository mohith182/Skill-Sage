===============================================================================

TIPS FOR BETTER ANSWERS:
1. Be specific: "How to solve quadratic equations?" vs "Help with math"
2. Include context: "I'm a beginner" or "This is for my exam"
3. Ask follow-ups: "Can you give me more examples?"
4. Request different formats: "Explain with diagrams" or "Give me code"

QUESTION FORMATS THAT WORK WELL:
- "How to..." (How to solve, How to build, How to understand)
- "What is..." (What is gravity, What is programming)
- "Why does..." (Why does this happen, Why is this important)
- "Explain..." (Explain quantum physics, Explain with examples)
- "Show me..." (Show me code, Show me examples)
- "Tell me about..." (Tell me about history, Tell me about science)
